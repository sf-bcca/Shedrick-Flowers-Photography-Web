## 2025-02-18 - Dark Mode Text Contrast
**Learning:** Using `dark:text-slate-500` for secondary text on dark backgrounds often fails contrast checks. `text-slate-400` provides a safer balance for both light and dark modes, or explicitly use `dark:text-slate-400`.
**Action:** When adding secondary text or counters, verify contrast in dark mode and prefer `text-slate-400` over darker shades.
